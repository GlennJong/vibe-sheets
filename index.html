How It Works

  1. Authenticate: Securely log in with your Google account.
  2. Create Sheet: With one click, the app generates a new Google Sheet configured as a database.

Why We Need Your Permission

To create and manage your database sheet, Vibe Sheets requires permission to access specific parts of your Google Account. We are transparent about what we use:

  • Google Spreadsheets: To create and modify the sheets that will act as your database.
  • Google Apps Script: To create the script that powers your API.
  • Google Drive: To manage the files created by the app and ensure everything is stored securely in your own Drive.

Your data is yours. Vibe Sheets runs entirely in your browser and does not store any of your personal information or files on our servers. Read our full Privacy Policy for more details.
